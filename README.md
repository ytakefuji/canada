The number of daily deaths due to the covid-19 can be used for measuring the success of the individual policies in the world.  Data in canada from Jan.22 2020 to Jan.8 2021 is used for examining the behavior of the pandemic. Policymakes need to control the pandemic by their policy based on the behavior of the pandemic. The prediction of the number of daily deaths in the near future plays a key role in the control.
In Figure, black line indicates the number of daily deaths while blue line shows the predicated number of daily deaths using 285 days based on the 10th degree polynomial. The red line indicates the predicted number of daily deaths changing the size of days from 100 days to 285 days from Jan. 8 2021 based on the 5th degree polynomial.
